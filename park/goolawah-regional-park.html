Goolawah Regional Park

Crescent Head, Kempsey, NSW

Point Plomer Rd, Crescent Head NSW 2440 (Delicate Campground Access)

Goolawah Regional Park is uniquely dog-friendly (on-leash) at the Delicate campground and Delicate Beach area. It offers a popular low-key beach camping experience. The reserve is ideal for coastal walks and fishing. Parking & Fees: Entry to the park is free. Camping fees apply for designated sites (Delicate campground).

For more pictures and directions refer to Google Maps.
