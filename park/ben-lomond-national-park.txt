The park features a range of ski slopes suitable for various skill levels, from beginners to advanced skiers. In addition to winter sports, the park offers several walking tracks, including the Summit Link Track, a 1 km walk that connects the alpine village to the steeper Summit Pass Track, and the Carr Villa to Alpine Village track, a 5 km walk that showcases the park's diverse alpine vegetation. The Ben Lomond Alpine Village provides accommodation options, including huts and a campground, catering to visitors seeking to immerse themselves in the alpine environment. The park is accessible via unsealed roads, with the final ascent up Jacobs Ladder being steep and winding. Visitors are advised to check road conditions before traveling, especially during the snow season, as snow chains are required between June and September. A valid parks pass is required for entry to Tasmania's national parks, and additional fees may apply for activities such as guided tours and shuttle bus services.
