Family Islands National Park

Mission Beach (island group), Cassowary Coast, QLD

Boat access from Clump Point, Mission Beach QLD 4852

Comprising Dunk, Bedarra and smaller islands, the park protects rainforest, reefs and sandy beaches. Dunk Island has walking tracks, campgrounds and day-use facilities; other islands are more remote and best reached by private boat or water taxi.

For more pictures and directions refer to Google Maps.
