Fort Lytton National Park

Wynnum North, Brisbane, QLD

160 South St, Lytton QLD 4178

Heritage-listed fort precinct with ramparts, tunnels and restored guns. Guided tours and reenactments offered on open days. Important site in Brisbane’s military history.

For more pictures and directions refer to Google Maps.
