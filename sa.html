South Australia

Discover South Australia's parks, from the Adelaide Hills and coastal reserves to Kangaroo Island's wildlife sanctuaries and outback conservation areas.

South Australia's parks showcase the state's unique character, from the wine regions and native bushland of the Adelaide Hills to the pristine beaches of the Fleurieu Peninsula and the remarkable wildlife of Kangaroo Island. Whether you're exploring coastal conservation parks, enjoying Adelaide's urban green spaces, or venturing into the outback, SA's parks offer diverse experiences for nature lovers and families alike.
