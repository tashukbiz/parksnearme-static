Dog Friendly Parks Perth: Top 10 Spots for Your Furry Friend

Perth offers an excellent selection of dog-friendly parks, perfect for exercise, socialisation, and outdoor adventures. From secure fenced enclosures with agility equipment to sprawling bushland reserves, Perth's parks cater to every dog's personality and needs. The city's sunny climate makes it ideal for year-round dog park visits.

Here's your guide to the best dog-friendly parks Perth has to offer.

Why Perth is Perfect for Dogs

Perth's sunny climate, extensive park network, and dog-loving community make it an exceptional city for dogs. Most parks offer secure fencing, water fountains, shade, and agility equipment, while many provide separate zones for small and large dogs. Best of all, most dog parks are free entry, so all you need is a leash, water, and waste bags.

Top 10 Dog Friendly Parks in Perth

1. Whiteman Park Dog Park

  • Location: Whiteman Park, Drumpellier Dr, Whiteman WA 6068
  • Why it's special: 2.5 hectares of fenced off-leash space with water points and agility obstacles.
  • Features: Separate areas for small dogs, popular for socialising, safe off-leash play, ample parking.

2. Aveley Dog Park

  • Location: The Parkway, Aveley WA 6069
  • Why it's special: Separate zones for small and large dogs with agility equipment and water bubblers.
  • Features: Fully enclosed for peace of mind, shaded areas, parking on-site.

3. Edgar Griffiths Dog Park

  • Location: 29 Garden Park Dr, Wanneroo WA 6065
  • Why it's special: Two secure enclosures with obstacle courses and sandy digging area.
  • Features: One area for small dogs, water fountains, lots of shade, well-maintained facilities.

4. Baldivis Enclosed Dog Park

  • Location: Acrasia Rd, Baldivis WA 6171
  • Why it's special: Large grassy areas with double-gated entry and agility equipment.
  • Features: Shaded seating, sandy digging zone, enclosures for both small and all dogs.

5. Kings Park and Botanic Garden

  • Location: Fraser Ave, Kings Park WA 6005
  • Why it's special: One of the world's largest inner-city parks with vast bushland and spectacular views (on-leash).
  • Features: 400 hectares of parkland, scenic city and river views, walking trails, picnic spots.

6. Ozone Dog Agility Park

  • Location: Plain St, East Perth WA 6004
  • Why it's special: Fully fenced with agility obstacles and spacious off-leash run.
  • Features: Water fountain, highly rated by local dog owners, central location.

7. Forrest Park

  • Location: Corner Walcott St & Curtis St, Mount Lawley WA 6050
  • Why it's special: Approved dog exercise area open 24/7 with plenty of shade.
  • Features: Seating, space for sports, peaceful mornings for strolls.

8. Woodville Reserve

  • Location: Corner Fitzgerald St & Farmer St, North Perth WA 6006
  • Why it's special: Fenced park with river views and BBQ facilities.
  • Features: Large lawn, close to tennis courts, great for family outings and picnics.

9. Piney Lakes Dog Play Park

  • Location: Leach Hwy, Winthrop WA 6150
  • Why it's special: Fifty acres of bushland with agility equipment and unlimited green views.
  • Features: Natural bushland setting, wetlands, native vegetation, plenty of space for sprints.

10. Charles Veryard Reserve

  • Location: 6 Bourke St, North Perth WA 6006
  • Why it's special: Off-leash areas with ample shade and floodlit sports grounds.
  • Features: Fully fenced playground nearby, suitable for family visits.

Tips for Visiting Perth Dog Parks

  • Check council websites: For up-to-date rules, possible bait warnings, and park hours.
  • Bring essentials: Water (Perth can get very hot), waste bags, and sun protection.
  • Sun safety: Visit early morning or late afternoon in summer to avoid peak heat.
  • Leash requirements: Dogs must be leashed outside of designated exercise zones or enclosed areas.
  • Facilities: Most parks have water, shade, and waste disposal, but bring your own bags just in case.
  • Beach options: Perth also offers several dog-friendly beaches and coastal areas for leashed strolls.

Other Great Dog Parks in Perth

Perth has many additional dog-friendly spaces worth exploring:

  • Carine Fenced Dog Park - Separate enclosures for small and large dogs with secure fencing.
  • St Clair Reserve Enclosed Dog Park - Paved walkways, water fountains, shaded seating in Rockingham.
  • Bold Park - Bushland setting with on-leash walking trails and ocean views.
  • Lake Monger - Popular lake circuit for on-leash walking (4km loop).

Final Thoughts

From secure fenced enclosures to natural bushland reserves, Perth offers excellent variety for dog owners. The city's sunny climate means you can enjoy outdoor activities with your dog year-round. Whether you have an energetic pup who needs agility equipment, a social dog who loves meeting friends, or a senior dog who prefers gentle walks, there's a perfect park waiting for you.

Grab your leash and enjoy exploring Perth with your furry companion!
